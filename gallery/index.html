₊✩ Educaté and Change Newsletter ✩₊

by Arely Ordonez-Chavez

This project was assigned in one my sociology courses where we chose an existing social issue that we were passionate about and were asked to create a psuedo newsletter in order to share the information we've gradually accumulated throughout the class. In my project, I chose to write about the struggles and under-representation for undocumented students and Latino communities. The purpose of my newsletter was to bring awareness to the lack of resources and attention for these students in order to reform the existing systems that marginalize and penalize these students, and to begin thinking about what programs and services we could implement in schools that can help these students succeed.

⊱✿⊰Perfection as Expectation⊱✿⊰

by Arely Ordonez-Chavez

This piece is a multimedia representation of the pressure of attaining perfection and how that shapes and impacts indivduals on a personal level. This project was composed of on sentence prompts posted on hitRECord, which invited indivduals to create written or visual (such as drawings or pictures) submissions on how the stress of perfection looks like to them. The final product was collage of different submissions by random hitRECord contributors which encapsulated how perfection can negatively impact people through the, sometimes unattainable, expectations to be perfect.
